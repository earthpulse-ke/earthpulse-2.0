    Our Mission

    Where sustainability meets action.

    EarthPulse is a youth-led environmental initiative based in Mombasa, Kenya, that addresses one of the countrys overlooked challenges: the mixing of organic food waste with regular trash. In many households, leftover food is discarded in landfills, where it releases methane, a potent greenhouse gas that contributes to climate change.

    Problems we are trying to solve

    Problem 1 - Current Waste Collection System in Kenya

    Waste is still collected the old way, everything mixed together at the source. Households and businesses dispose of paper, plastic, organic waste, and e-waste in a single stream.

    By the time waste reaches collection companies, it is contaminated and difficult to separate. Valuable materials like paper and plastic lose their quality when mixed with organic waste, making proper recycling inefficient, costly, or even impossible.

    As a result, time, money, and resources are wasted on segregation, and much of the recyclable waste loses its value entirely.

    Problem 2

    Organic food waste becomes a major problem when it is mixed with other types of trash, as it releases moisture and bacteria that contaminate recyclable materials like paper and plastic, making them unusable. This not only reduces recycling efficiency but also forces more waste into landfills, where organic matter decomposes and produces methane, a harmful greenhouse gas. Instead of being reused as compost or energy, valuable resources are lost, increasing both environmental damage and waste management costs.

    Problem 3

    Farmers spend a large portion of their income on fuel and fertilizers. Lack of affordable local alternatives forces reliance on expensive imports. The current system creates a financial drain on the agricultural sector.

    Our Projects

    Aerobins

    Aerobins are small-scale composting systems designed to break down organic waste efficiently using aerobic (oxygen-based) decomposition. They provide a controlled environment where food scraps and biodegradable materials decompose quickly into nutrient-rich compost, reducing waste sent to landfills while producing a valuable resource for soil improvement.

    Biogas Solution

    EarthPulse focuses on developing a sustainable solution to convert organic waste into biogas, turning everyday food waste into a clean and renewable source of energy. By using simple biodigestion methods, we aim to reduce landfill waste, lower methane emissions, and provide an eco-friendly alternative to traditional fuels while promoting awareness about responsible waste management in our community.

    Binman

    BinMan is a platform designed to improve waste management by promoting proper segregation at the source. It helps households and businesses sort their waste into categories like paper, plastic, e-waste, and organic waste, and connects them directly with specialized recycling companies. By ensuring each type of waste is handled correctly from the start, BinMan reduces contamination, improves efficiency, and maximizes the value of recyclable materials.
